title: Acknowledgements
---

Futhark has received contributions in terms of code, testing, or feedback from
many individual programmers, for which we are very grateful.

In addition, the Futhark project is grateful for the received financial or other
support from the following organisations.

[The Independent Research Fund Denmark](https://dff.dk) supported Futhark via
the following projects:

* *FUTHARK: Functional Technology for High-performance Architectures*

* *Deep Probabilistic Programming for Protein Structure Prediction*

[DIREC](https://direc.dk/) provided funding for a student programmer through the
project [Ergonomic and Practical Effect
Systems](https://direc.dk/ergonomic-practical-effect-systems/).

[The STIBO Foundation](https://www.stibofonden.dk/) has provided funding for a
student programmer to work on the WebGPU backend.

[NVIDIA](http://nvidia.com/) has donated several GPUs as part of the [Academic
Grant
Program](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/industries/higher-education-research/academic-grant-program/).

[The University of Copenhagen](https://ku.dk) supports Futhark by employing its
core developers at [DIKU](https://diku.dk), and through the following projects:

* The Data+ grant *High-Performance Land Change Assessment*.
